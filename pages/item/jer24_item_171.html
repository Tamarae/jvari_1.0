cross reference:ნარევი ტაბულა II
key figures:Patron Shuria (donor), Ivane Ksareshvili (agent).
item classification:foundation_donation
liturgical calendar order:020
financial summary:Donation of 101 mutqali of gold.
donation context:This donation was made during a period of hardship caused by Persians, similar to the context of item 145.
scribal note:The scribe wrote 'სუსლსი' (suslsi), likely for 'სასულე' (sasule, for the soul), and 'გაიკითხვი' (gaikithkhvi) for 'განიკითხვის' (ganikithkhvis).
