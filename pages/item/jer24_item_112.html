financial summary:A large and itemized donation totaling over 150 florin, including: 100 florin primary donation; 32 florin for redeeming pledged items; 3 florin for registration; 2 florin to Katamon; 2 florin to the abbot; 4 florin to four priests (1 each); 3 florin for agapes; 2 florin to Shila; 1 florin (each) to other Georgian monasteries. Pilipe himself added 1 florin for a lamp at Shila, 4 florin for candles/incense, and 2.5 florin for oil at Golgotha. The donation also funded extensive construction and furnishing works.
donation details:The foundation included significant material contributions beyond cash: redeeming pledged liturgical vessels, extensive construction (whitewashing, door installation, plastering), and furnishing a church with curtains, lamps, and candlesticks.
name conversion:The commemorated person, Tamaz, had taken the religious name Tadeoz.
scribal note:The manuscript contains a scribal error, 'ფრურისა' (prurisa), which has been corrected to 'ფლურისა' (plurisa).
